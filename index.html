The Scribe

Elias Thorne.

"Longevity is our metric. A tattoo should feel like it has always resided beneath your skin."

With over 12 years of professional practice spanning Copenhagen and London, Elias Thorne has pioneered the Editorial Fine Line methodology. His work is recognized for its architectural precision and narrative depth.

Elias founded Inkwell Studio to bridge the gap between traditional craftsmanship and digital delivery systems, ensuring a seamless, permanent experience for the modern collector.
