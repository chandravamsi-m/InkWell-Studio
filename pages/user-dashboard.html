Biological Preservation & Narrative Care Protocol

The Initial Phase (0-48 Hours)

Leave the medical-grade dressing on for at least 24 hours. Once removed, cleanse gently with non-perfumed, neutral pH soap.

Regenerative Care (Day 3-14)

  • Apply thin layer of Studio Balm 3x daily
  • Avoid direct sunlight or submersion
  • Wear loose, breathable natural fabrics

Critical Assistance

If you experience excessive swelling, localized heat, or persistent discharge beyond the 72-hour mark, dispatch an immediate message to the studio.
