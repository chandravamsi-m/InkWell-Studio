Studio Protocol.

When do the inquiry books open?
We open books quarterly. The best way to secure a spot is to register a client account and join the waitlist. Priority is given to narratives that align with our current architectural focus.
Is the studio open for walk-ins?
Inkwell is a private, appointment-only sanctuary. We do not accept walk-ins to maintain the clinical focus and editorial calm throughout our deep-narrative sessions.
What is the studio guest policy?
To preserve the quiet, focused atmosphere of our studio, we ask that you come alone to your session. We treat every appointment as a private exhibition.
