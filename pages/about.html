Spatial Context

The Hackney
Sanctuary.

Inkwell Studio was born in a former industrial warehouse in Hackney. The space was chosen for its bones—raw concrete, north-facing lighting, and a profound sense of isolation from the chaos of the city.

We treat the physical environment as a critical component of the dermal archiving process. Curated lighting, noise-isolated session rooms, and a minimalist aesthetic ensure that every commission receives absolute focus.
